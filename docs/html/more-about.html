Our Team

Emma Price

Emma Price is a multidisciplinary graphic designer and illustrator living in the UK. She has created the logo and brand identity for Best Jackett Press, worked with the team to create the super stylish NOCTERRA Kickstarter editions and designed the ongoing series. She's also created logos and design for other books in the Best Jackett range including BARNSTORMERS, CLEAR and WE HAVE DEMONS.

Her previous credits include Coffin Bound, Savage Town (Image Comics), Motherlands (Vertigo) and Stronghold (Aftershock Comics), to name a few.

Will Dennis

Will Dennis was an editor at Vertigo/DC Entertainment for more than fifteen years, specializing in genre fiction comics and graphic novels. His award-winning titles include 100 Bullets, Y: The Last Man, Scalped, DMZ, Joker and many more. Since then, he has worked as a freelance editor for Image Comics, comiXology, DC Entertainment and also wrote The Art of Jock for Insight Editions. He lives in Brooklyn, NY.

Tyler Jennes

Tyler Jennes is an assistant editor, working with Will Dennis for the past year on comic titles by creators like Jeff Lemire, Rick Remender, Brian Azzarello, Jason Aaron, and yes, Scott Snyder. You can check out what he's currently working on by going to Will's website, bespokecomics.com, which he also designed, but doesn't want to sound braggy about it or anything.

THE MOST IMPORTANT COMIC BOOK ON EARTH, an environmental benefit anthology featuring his first written works, is set to release internationally in late October.
